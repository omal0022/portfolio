Bio

Hi, I’m Merrill. An Interactive Media Design student with a flair for creative expression. Having already worked in the fields of graphic design and digital illustration, I found myself yearning for a more diverse skill set, prompting my decision to pursue higher education where I rediscovered my love of learning. Outside of the classroom, I like to channel my creativity into textile arts, spending my free time sewing, quilting, and knitting.
